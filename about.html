Our Team


Rob Dickinson

Cofounder, Engineering

Rob has spent nearly his entire career dealing with the complexities of building and supporting online services. As an engineer at several Colorado-based Internet startups, Rob has seen what happens when a web API or application doesn't work as tested. As principal architect of Xaffire, Rob led development of a scalable tracing platform that gives deep visibility into web operations. As Xaffire grew to be purchased by Quest Software and then by Dell, Rob has worked with hundreds of customers, including some of the largest e-businesses, to make web applications better. Rob left Dell in 2015, prior to starting resurface.io.
